About CyberNaach


WELCOME TO CYBERNAACH


The first of its kind, CyberNaach brings the same love and passion the collegiate South Asian community has for fusion dance into an entirely virtual, COVID-safe setting.

With our comp, it doesn’t matter if your dancers can't return to your city and are located all over the world. Can’t meet for practice? No problemo. CyberNaach gives your dancers the ability to represent your team on a competitive platform from the safety (and luxury) of their homes.

How Does This Work? Each week, teams will "battle" another team. In the initial rounds, teams will be assigned 1 or 2 segments of a mix (roughly ~40 seconds per segment and either 1 or 2 dancers depending on size of the team pool). Each team then has 1 WEEK to submit each dancer’s videos.

To Reiterate: Only 1 dancer on screen per segment. Your team will receive one or two segments to submit at the end of Week 1. You will be going head to head with another team that will be assigned the same segment(s). Your videos will be judged and the winner of your matchup will move on to the next round.

All videos will be judged by our panel of judges , and the rubric can be found here .

Who Can Register? Teams must be comprised of 7-9 members who are currently students at any college! So get any of your 7 of your dancer friends together, or compete with members of a team you're already on! Please see CyberNaach Official Rules for more detailed information.

What’s the Catch? No catch. Registration fee is $50 per team to enter the competition, and that’s it! (If teams would like to purchase swag, that may be an additional fee. We may also provide swag at no additional cost to all teams if enough teams participate in the competition!)

Prizes
First Place: $500 | Second Place: $200 | Third Place: $100

When?

Sign Ups close 9/30, Registration materials are due 10/7, and the comp will start 10/12. Please check out the full timeline for more information.
