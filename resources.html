Registration Information

After submitting your initial sign up form , there are only TWO items that must be submitted by teams for official registration for CyberNaach 2020.

  1. $50 Registration Fee via Venmo
  2. Names, Contact Info, and Government ID for the 7-9 dancers competing on your roster.
The Venmo Handle and Roster Registration Form for steps 1 and 2 will be given to teams via the contact info provided on the initial sign up form.
